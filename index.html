TEBB AND THE CHISTORBES


Someone stole people's faces! Will any hero be able to collect the 100 chistorbes to recover the face of the whole world? Platform game inspired by Super Meat Boy, Celeste and VVVVVV. Simple controls: Move, run and jump.


This game wants to be challenging but fair, dynamic, fast recovery after death and frequent checkpoints, but still present a challenge.


If it becomes too difficult, you can activate an "Assist Mode" in the options menu to avoid dying and be able to levitate. The current version has world 1 made with 20 levels, the final version is planned to be 5 worlds with 100 levels in total. It is expected to have its own soundtrack, more levels, more content in general, and the possibility of porting to consoles. There are probably characters that in some levels will change mobility, such as flying or climbing walls.















GAMEPLAYS AND SETTINGS


During this time, I watched several people play to see what could be improved, measure the difficulty, see that it is optimized and works on several PCs.


One of the biggest challenges was adjusting the difficulty. Try to keep it challenging, fun, without being overwhelming or unfair or too easy. For this, watch many people play. If I saw that a lot of people couldn't beat the same level, I would add indications (for example the characters that remind you to run) or reduce the difficulty, adding platforms, shrinking hitboxes, etc.


If it becomes too difficult, you can activate an "Assist Mode" in the options menu to avoid dying and be able to levitate. With this mode activated, you will not be able to get the 3-star rating at the end of the world, and you will not be able to unlock some Steam achievements, the rest will not change.


Also, some levels have optional collectibles (10 in total) somewhat difficult, but optional. These 10 collectibles unlock another 3 levels, also difficult. All worlds are planned to have 3 unlockable bonus levels with collectibles.


Thank you very much to all the people who played! both on Youtube and Twitch, I was able to watch their reactions, see their feedback on the experience, and make a better game. I will keep working on improving the gameplay and details.

About the Author




My name is Andres, but I use Xouxer as a nickname, and I appear as Andru Caba in the game.


I've been making flash animations and small games for some time. The first version of "Tebb" was in 2016 and can be downloaded on GameJolt. Personally, I really like platform games, which don't require a lot of exploration or resource gathering to advance, and with frequent checkpoints. The best games that I remember with those characteristics are "Super meat boy" and "VVVVVV" sometimes while playing those I imagined some punctual mechanic that I wanted to add. When I came up with enough mechanics, I started thinking about how to create my own game. I am currently studying programming, such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
